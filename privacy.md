    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-text mt-10 mb-4 pb-2 border-b border-border">1. Introduction</h2>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      This Privacy Policy explains how Eltrix ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use our open-source Matrix homeserver software or visit our website.
    </p>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      Eltrix is open-source software that can be self-hosted. This policy applies to our website and documentation. If you use Eltrix software hosted by a third party, their privacy policy applies.
    </p>

    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-text mt-10 mb-4 pb-2 border-b border-border">2. Information We Collect</h2>

    <h3 class="text-xl font-semibold text-text mt-8 mb-3">Website Visitors</h3>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      When you visit our website, we may collect:
    </p>
    <ul class="text-text mb-4 list-disc list-inside space-y-1">
      <li>IP address (anonymized)</li>
      <li>Browser type and version</li>
      <li>Pages visited and time spent</li>
      <li>Referring website</li>
    </ul>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      We use privacy-respecting analytics that do not track individual users or use cookies for tracking.
    </p>

    <h3 class="text-xl font-semibold text-text mt-8 mb-3">Self-Hosted Eltrix</h3>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      Eltrix software, when self-hosted, does not send any data to us. All data remains on your server under your control.
    </p>

    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-text mt-10 mb-4 pb-2 border-b border-border">3. How We Use Information</h2>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      We use collected information to:
    </p>
    <ul class="text-text mb-4 list-disc list-inside space-y-1">
      <li>Improve our website and documentation</li>
      <li>Understand how our documentation is used</li>
      <li>Ensure website security and prevent abuse</li>
    </ul>

    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-text mt-10 mb-4 pb-2 border-b border-border">4. Data Sharing</h2>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:
    </p>
    <ul class="text-text mb-4 list-disc list-inside space-y-1">
      <li>When required by law</li>
      <li>To protect our rights or safety</li>
      <li>With service providers who assist in operating our website (under strict data protection agreements)</li>
    </ul>

    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-text mt-10 mb-4 pb-2 border-b border-border">5. Data Retention</h2>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      We retain analytics data for a maximum of 12 months. Server logs are retained for 30 days.
    </p>

    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-text mt-10 mb-4 pb-2 border-b border-border">6. Your Rights</h2>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
    </p>
    <ul class="text-text mb-4 list-disc list-inside space-y-1">
      <li>Access your personal data</li>
      <li>Correct inaccurate data</li>
      <li>Delete your data</li>
      <li>Object to processing</li>
      <li>Data portability</li>
    </ul>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@eltrix.dev.
    </p>

    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-text mt-10 mb-4 pb-2 border-b border-border">7. Security</h2>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:
    </p>
    <ul class="text-text mb-4 list-disc list-inside space-y-1">
      <li>HTTPS encryption for all web traffic</li>
      <li>Regular security audits</li>
      <li>Access controls and authentication</li>
    </ul>

    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-text mt-10 mb-4 pb-2 border-b border-border">8. Changes to This Policy</h2>
    <p class="text-text leading-relaxed mb-4">
      We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
    </p>
